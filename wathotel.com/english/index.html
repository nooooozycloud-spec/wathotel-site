ONSEN

Natrium-Hydrogen carbonate/chloride spring (hot spring with hypotonic alkaline level)
*HEATING- Natural Heating as well as artificial regulation of the water temperature.
*Circulation/Filtration: We use circulators to maintain the water temperature of the Onsen.
*Chlorine Treatment- Regular Chlorine treatment ensures protection of hot spring resources and also helps in maintaining hygiene.

We use the original spring water of ‘ Hidamari no Yu’ which is located in the northern part of Takayama.
